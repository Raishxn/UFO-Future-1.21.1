# Energy Cells

<BlockImage id="ufo:ufo_energy_cell" scale="4"></BlockImage>

The **Event Horizon Energy Cell** stores **1G AE** and works as the large power buffer for late UFO systems.

<BlockImage id="ufo:quantum_energy_cell" scale="4"></BlockImage>

The **Quantum Energy Cell** is the infinite-tier AE power cell and always reports full stored energy in its tooltip.
