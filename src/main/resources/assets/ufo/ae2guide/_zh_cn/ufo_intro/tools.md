# UFO Tools

UFO Future includes transformable energy tools and utility items for multiblocks.

## Tool Line

- Energy powered instead of durability based
- Several forms such as Pickaxe, Hammer, Sword, Greatsword, Axe, Shovel, Hoe, Bow and Staff
- Stored energy is kept during transformation

## Structure Scanner

- Validates multiblock structures
- Highlights missing or invalid blocks
- Prints positions and expected parts
- In Creative, **Shift + Right Click** auto-builds the shell
